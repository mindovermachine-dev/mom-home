## This is MoM's weekly Office Hour meeting

### Pre meeting steps:

- Set up a free account on [our slack](https://slack.mindovermachine.dk) if you haven't already
- Join the channel [`#mom-office-hours`](https://mindovermachine-dk.slack.com/archives/C0B4SUYJW4R)
- Add agenda items in that channel if you have any (it fine if you don't have any - join anyway)

### Get this as a calendar invite

You can join the mail group `office-hours@mindovermachine.dk` to get the calendar invites for the office hours.

Visit [office-hours.mindovermachine.dk](https://office-hours.mindovermachine.dk) It will redirect you to the google group and you can click the "Join group" button - from here on you will get invites to alle office-hour events.

At any time feel free to ignore or reject the invites. To leave the group use the ([same link](https://office-hours.mindovermachine.dk)).

### At the time of the event

We will start a huddle in the channel - join it, or jump straight to it.

If you need more details see the "[Office Hours](https://mindovermachine-dk.slack.com/docs/T09DW7D2W4Q/F0B5KDN9YBA)" tab in the `#mom-office-hours` slack channel - or just pop a question in the same channel
