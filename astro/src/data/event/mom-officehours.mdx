## This is MoM's weekly Office Hour meeting

The meeting is run as a [huddle](https://app.slack.com/huddle/T09DW7D2W4Q/C0B4SUYJW4R) in our slack channel [`#mom-office-hours`](https://mindovermachine-dk.slack.com/archives/C0B4SUYJW4R) and is open to anyone who wants to join.

This meeting is an integrated part of our _Community of Practice_. We advocate full transparency and _Legitimate Peripheral Participation_. In short: no matter what you interest or incentive is: You are so welcome 🤩.

**You should join if:**

- You have an idea you want to pitch and get feedback on
- You want to ask for help on a project you are working on
- You are already in one of the MoM circles and want to share your progress or get feedback
- You don't get what regenerative software is or why it matters — and want to learn more
- You are just curious and don't want anything in particular — just curious...

### Lean Coffee style

The meeting is are run in a [Lean Coffee](https://leancoffee.org/) style. This means that we will start with a blank agenda and the participants will propose topics to discuss. The topics will be voted on and the most popular ones will be discussed first. Each topic will have a timebox of 8 minutes and we will use a timer to keep track of time. If a topic is not finished within the timebox, we will vote on whether to continue or move on to the next topic.

### Dont miss a beat - get the invites in your calendar!

We have set up a open google group [`office-hours.mindovermachine.dk`](https://office-hours.mindovermachine.dk) which is invited to all office hour meetings. If you join the group, you will see the invites along with all your other calendar events. You can ignore or reject the invites if you don't want to attend a specific meeting.

If you would like to get the invites set to an email address which is _not_ a google account. Or you can help yourself: Simply 'wrap' on your current email address is a free google account. It's harmless and non-intrusive, it simply allows you to use Google's OAuth and services with any email address you have (not email obviously, but stuff like _drive, photos, groups, gemini_ etc...).
