To-Peer or not-to Peer: centralization in networks happens when its peers lose direct contact, increasing hub pressures and reducing resilience \- the opposite of what the Web was invented for. Let's revive social hypermedia by linking to each other's content, and crawl the network to see what we are made of\! We might even contribute to each other's projects. Can I get your IPv6-number?
