## Purpose

The committee for good fund management publishes every year [a form](https://godfondsledelse.dk/redegoerelse), which contains recommendations and requirements for good fund management. The form is an important tool for the board in its work to ensure that the fund is managed and controlled in a way that complies with the articles of association, legislation and good practice:

> _"Commercial foundations that are covered by the Norwegian Business Foundations Act must report annually on how they relate to the recommendations, regardless of whether they are followed or not..."_

_The committee for good fund management_

MoM has expanded the concept and calls it an _annual ethical assessment_, where the scheme for good fund management is included together with additional self-imposed ethical considerations, which the board deals with in its annual review.

The annual ethical assessment is published on the foundation's website and is included in the annual report, so stakeholders can see how the foundation relates to good fund management and the ethical assessments the board has made in the past year.

The full, current list of recommendations is maintained by the Committee for good Fund Management and can be found here:

- [Committee statement form](https://godfondsledelse.dk/redegoerelse)

MoM does not render the entire schema locally to avoid obsolete duplicates. This means that the board always works with the latest official version.
