## Purpose

This document brings together the fund's annual cycle in a practical rhythm for the whole year.

The purpose is to ensure continuity in operations, supervision, documentation and decisions across strategy, finances, risks and ethics.

## Frame

The board holds at least four ordinary meetings annually in accordance with the rules of procedure.

The annual wheel is a management tool. It can be adjusted if necessary, but the minimum tasks must be completed every year.

The board has the formal responsibility for adoption, prioritization and supervision.

The management is responsible for planning, execution, reporting and follow-up.

Employees contribute with data bases, professional deliverables, learning and documentation, which make the management's decisions robust.

## Roles and responsibilities

The board determines direction, makes decisions and supervises progress and risks.

The management translates decisions into plans, coordinates deliveries across the organization and reports to the board in a timely manner.

Employees provide professional documentation, status, quality data and improvement proposals as input to the management's reporting and the board's decisions.

## Quarterly rhythm

### 1st quarter

The board deals with the year-end, audit matters and the overall status for the previous year.

The management presents the annual status of operations, finances, risks and significant learning points.

Employees contribute with documented status from projects, operations and quality work.

The board approves key focus areas for the new year, including strategic priorities and overall distribution direction.

The board updates the risk picture and determines concrete follow-up points for the rest of the year.

### 2nd quarter

The board follows up progress in strategy, finances and operations.

The management implements the decided priorities and reports any deviations with proposals for action.

Employees provide measurement points for quality, capacity and learning so that priorities can be adjusted early.

The board reviews capital management and assesses whether the practice continues to match the purpose in the short and long term.

The board makes a mid-year adjustment of priorities if the conditions have changed significantly.

### 3rd quarter

The board is working on planning for next year, including resources, risks and governance needs.

The management prepares presentations on targets, capacity needs and expected investments for next year.

Employees contribute with suggestions for improvements, competence needs and operational experiences from the year.

The board evaluates the cooperation with the executive board and the quality of the reporting framework.

The board reviews any need to update instructions and policies.

The board plans the year's hearing in the initiative circle, including themes, decision needs and any processes for the right of appointment to the board.

### 4th quarter

The board carries out an annual self-evaluation of the board's work and the person in charge's management of the board.

The management carries out a management evaluation of cooperation, reporting and implementation power.

Employees contribute structured feedback on collaboration, workflows and opportunities for improvement.

The representative body holds an annual meeting or consultation meeting, and recommendations are considered at the next ordinary board meeting.

The board carries out the annual ethical review based on the Committee's report form.

The board adopts a provisional annual plan for the coming year and decides on follow-up points for the 1st quarter.

## Fixed topics at each ordinary meeting

The agenda must at least include formalities, decision points, reporting from the executive board and follow-up on outstanding matters.

The board reviews the financial status, significant risks and any issues of competence.

New additions to the audit protocol are processed and logged.

The management's follow-up on previous decisions is reviewed with clear responsibility, deadline and status.

## Documentation and update

The annual wheel is reviewed at least once a year and updated according to the board's decision.

In the event of changes, the document is updated with clear justification, so that continuity and transparency are maintained.
