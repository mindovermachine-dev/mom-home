## Slack

To stay updated on our everything, like a news feed, but where you can also contribute to the dialogue, you can join our Slack channel `#mind-over-machine`:

<Steps>

1. **Go to [`slack.devopsdays.dk`](https://slack.devopsdays.dk)**<br/>
   Create an account if you don't already have one and log in

2. **Find the [`#mind-over-machine`](https://devopsdays-dk.slack.com/archives/C0B09F5MTAA) channel**<br/>
   Introduce yourself and how you are interested in contributing or what you would like to learn more about.

3. **Be active in the dialogue**<br/>
   Share your thoughts and ideas, and participate in the various discussions and projects that take place in the channel.

4. **If you feel like being more involved**<br/>
   ... and could see yourself taking a formal role then contact us directly on hey@mindovermachine.dk.

</Steps>

The slack is open to everyone, and we encourage you to join and contribute to the dialogue, even if you are not yet sure how you want to contribute or what role you want to take. We are happy to have you as part of the community and look forward to hearing your thoughts and ideas.

:::note
Our slack is still named _DevOpsDays-DK_ as the foundation itself is still in progress of being registered with the Danish authorities. We will rename the slack once the foundation is registered, but in the meantime, you can find us in the `#mind-over-machine` channel on the DevOpsDays-DK slack.
:::

## Mailing list

We also have a mailing list for those who prefer email communication.

You can subscribe to the mailing list by sending an email to [`hey@mindovermachine.dk`](mailto:hey@mindovermachine.dk?subject=Subscribe%20to%20Mind%20over%20Machine%20mailing%20list) with the subject "Subscribe to Mind over Machine mailing list".

Please note that the mailing list is currently used for engagement and active involvement of advisors. If you are interested in joining the mailing list, please add a few words about your background and how you are interested in contributing to the foundation in the email.

We encourage you to — also — join our Slack channel for the latest updates and discussions.

## Office Hours

We are hosting regular office hours where you can drop in and have a chat with us about anything related to Mind over Machine, regenerative software development, or just to say hi and get to know us better.

<kicker>"_The office hours are held every week on Tuesdays 8:30 AM._"</kicker>

The office hours are open to everyone, and they run as a [huddle](https://app.slack.com/huddle/T09DW7D2W4Q/C0B4SUYJW4R) (video call) in the dedicated [`#mom-office-hours`](https://devopsdays-dk.slack.com/archives/C0B4SUYJW4R) channel on our Slack. You can join the huddle at any time during the office hours, and you can stay for as long as you like.
